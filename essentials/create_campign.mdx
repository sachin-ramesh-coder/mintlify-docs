# Create your first campaign

<img className="rounded-md" src="https://github.com/wato-ai/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/first%20campaign%20assets/main1.png?raw=true" />

Here is a step by step guide to create your first campaign.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Campaigns tab" icon="arrow-pointer">
    Click on the campaigns tab on your home screen.

    <img className="rounded-md" src="https://github.com/wato-ai/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/first%20campaign%20assets/asset-1.jpg?raw=true" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill details" icon="memo-circle-check">
    Fill in the details of the campaign and watch it live on the screen beside.

    <img className="rounded-md" src="https://github.com/wato-ai/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/first%20campaign%20assets/asset-2.jpg?raw=true" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Hyperlink" icon="link">
    Do not forget to hyperlink your destination link. It will help you with detailed tracking and measurement.

    <img className="rounded-md" src="https://github.com/wato-ai/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/first%20campaign%20assets/asset-3.jpg?raw=true"  />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule / Send Now" icon="calendar-days">
    You can schedule the campaign later or send it now.

    <img className="rounded-md" src="https://github.com/wato-ai/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/first%20campaign%20assets/asset-4.jpg?raw=true" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Community" icon="square-check">
    Select you community/ communities where you want to send the campaign to.

    <img className="rounded-md" src="https://github.com/wato-ai/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/first%20campaign%20assets/asset-5.jpg?raw=true" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Community" icon="people-group">
    Post delivery you can measure the success of your campaign through the campaigns tab by selecting your campaign.

    <img className="rounded-md" src="https://github.com/wato-ai/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/first%20campaign%20assets/asset-6.jpg?raw=true" />
  </Step>
</Steps>
