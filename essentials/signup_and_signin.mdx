# Starting up at wato

<img className="rounded-md" src="https://github.com/wato-ai/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/signup%20and%20signin/asset%201.png?raw=true" />

## Here is a step-by-step process for you to start using Wato:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Account" icon="user-plus">
    Create your account here: [signup](https://wato.ai/site/signup)
<Tip>Make sure to contact your account manager or drop a mail at [info@wato.ai](mailto:info@wato.ai) after signup <br />
So that we can setup your **server**! </Tip>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Get a number" icon="hashtag">
    Get a new number: Wato works with personal/business numbers, not Business APIs. You can also use an existing number if it is already being used for groups/communities.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Server Setup" icon="server">
    Server setup: Within the next four hours of your sign-up, a new server will be set up to enable your services. You will receive an email to confirm the same.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in" icon="right-to-bracket">
    Sign-in at : [signin](https://wato.ai/site/signin) and Scan the QR code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Community" icon="people-group">
    Start creating your communities!
  </Step>
  </Steps>



<Note>In case of any queries, feel free to contact us. write us to [info@wato.ai](mailto:info@wato.ai)</Note>
